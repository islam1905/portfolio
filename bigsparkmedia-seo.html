Jumper Zhang E-Commercial Website

A well-known designer, Jumper Zhang, requests an e-commerce site to operate in Canada.

Project Goals

The requested website will be an e-commerce system where customers can place orders and make payments. Additionally, the website should include information about the company and have a modern, minimalist design.

Target Audience

The target audience for this e-commerce website includes tech-savvy online shoppers who value a seamless and secure purchasing experience. They appreciate modern, minimalist designs that offer an intuitive and clutter-free interface, allowing for quick navigation and hassle-free transactions. These consumers, ranging from busy professionals to mobile users, seek efficiency and convenience in online shopping while also valuing transparency and brand credibility. By providing a sleek design, clear company information, and a smooth checkout process, the website will appeal to both local and global customers looking for a trustworthy and user-friendly shopping platform.
